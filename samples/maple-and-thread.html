Our commitment

Better made, by people we know

We’d rather make less and make it right. Every step — from fibre to your front door — is chosen to tread lightly and treat people fairly.

Natural materials

Organic cotton, GOTS-certified merino, linen, and recycled cashmere. No virgin synthetics — ever. Every fibre is traceable to its mill.

Ethical making

We partner with 14 family-run workshops across Portugal, Peru, and BC. Living wages, safe hours, and a name on every hangtag.

Lower carbon

Sea-freight first, plastic-free packaging, and verified offsets on every parcel. We publish our footprint each year — no greenwashing.
