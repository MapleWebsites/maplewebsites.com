What we treat

Care for every stage of recovery

From a weekend tweak to a major surgery, our registered physiotherapists build a plan that meets you where you are.

Sports injury

Sprains, strains, runner's knee and return-to-sport programs. We rebuild strength so the injury doesn't come back.

Most common · ACL, ankle, shoulder

Back & neck pain

Disc irritation, sciatica, tension headaches and posture-related pain. Hands-on relief plus a plan to keep it away.

Manual therapy · mobility work

Post-surgical rehab

Structured recovery after joint replacement, ACL repair, rotator cuff and more — coordinated with your surgeon's protocol.

Guided · milestone-based

ICBC & MVA recovery

Pre-approved care after a motor-vehicle accident. We handle the ICBC paperwork so you can focus on healing.

Direct ICBC billing · no upfront cost

Dry needling / IMS

Intramuscular stimulation to release deep muscular knots and calm chronic tension that stretching alone won't reach.

Performed by certified physios

Pelvic health

Discreet, expert care for pre/postnatal recovery, incontinence and pelvic pain in our private treatment rooms.

Private rooms · all genders

Insurance & direct billing

We bill your insurer directly — not your patience

Most of our patients pay little or nothing out of pocket. We submit your claim electronically at the front desk, so you only cover what your plan doesn't. ICBC and MVA claims are pre-approved — bring your claim number and we handle the rest.
