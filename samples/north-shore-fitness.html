The Programs

Five ways to get after it

Coach-led, capped class sizes, and a community that drags you up the hill on the days you'd rather sleep in.

Trail Run Club

Sunrise and dusk runs on Grouse, Fromme and the Baden-Powell. Pace groups from couch-to-trail to sub-40 10K.

OutdoorAll pacesMon · Wed · Sat

Strength Lab

Barbell-led progressive overload. Squat, hinge, press, pull — programmed in 8-week blocks with real numbers to chase.

Most bookedCoachedDaily

HIIT & Conditioning

45 minutes of intervals, sleds, rowers and assault bikes. Built to spike your engine and burn long after you leave.

High intensity45 minTue · Thu

Mobility & Recovery

Breathwork, banded flow and soft-tissue work to keep your hips, ankles and shoulders trail-ready year round.

Low impactBeginner okSun

Good to Know

Questions, answered

Everything you need before you walk through the doors. Still stuck? The front desk is one message away.

Yes. Monthly Unlimited members can freeze up to 3 months per year, and Annual members up to 4 — for travel, injury, or life. Freezes are free, activate instantly in your member app, and your billing simply pauses until you're back. No paperwork, no penalty.
Just trainers, water, and a willingness to sweat. We provide towels, lockers, mats and all equipment. For Trail Run Club, wear trail or road runners and dress for the weather — North Shore mornings bite. Showers, hairdryers and a cold plunge are on site for after.
Absolutely — most of our members started exactly there. Every class is scalable, coaches give you a personal version of each movement, and our Trail Run Club has a dedicated couch-to-trail pace group. Your first class is free so you can find your footing with zero pressure.
Day Passes are a single one-time visit. The 10-Class Pack gives you ten credits valid for six months, redeemable on any program and even transferable to a friend. Memberships bill on a cycle (monthly or annual) and unlock unlimited classes. Buy any of them online above — they land in your account instantly.
We're a 4-minute walk from Lonsdale Quay SeaBus and on the 230 and 232 bus routes. Members get two hours of free covered parking under the building, and there's a secure bike room with a wash station for those who ride or run in.
Monthly Unlimited is genuinely month-to-month — cancel from the app with 14 days' notice, no fees, no awkward phone calls. Annual members can transfer or pause their plan; if your circumstances change drastically, talk to us and we'll find a fair solution.
