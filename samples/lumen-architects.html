The studio

Four convictions that shape every commission.

We are small by design — a tight studio of architects and interior designers who stay with a project from the first sketch to the final handle.

01 — Light first

Daylight is the first material

We model the sun before we draw a wall. Every plan is tested for how morning, noon and evening light will move across it through the seasons.

02 — Proportion

Restraint over ornament

A room is right when nothing more can be removed. We resolve buildings through proportion, rhythm and the honest meeting of materials.

03 — Material

Built to weather beautifully

Concrete, oak, lime plaster, blackened steel — we choose materials that age into something better and detail them to last generations.

04 — Place

Rooted in its landscape

A building should belong to its site and its climate. We design with the grain of the land, the local light, and the way a place is actually lived in.
