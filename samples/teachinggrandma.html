How it works

Three easy steps. No jargon, no pressure, and a real person on the other end the whole way through.

1. Book a free chat

Pick a topic and a time that suits you. The first 20-minute chat is free, friendly, and completely no-obligation.

2. Learn together

We meet by phone or video — whichever feels easier — and go at your pace. We share our screen and walk through every tap.

3. Feel confident

You leave with simple written notes in large print, and you can always book a friendly refresher whenever you need one.
