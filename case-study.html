A custom rebuild that tripled online reservations in 90 days.

Harvest Moon Bistro had a loyal following but a slow, hard-to-find website that buried its booking link. Maple rebuilt it from scratch — custom design, local SEO, an AI chatbot, and analytics wired in — and turned the site into the restaurant's busiest table.

The story

A great kitchen with an invisible website

The food was never the problem. Getting people to find, trust, and book the table online was.

The challenge

Hungry diners couldn't find — or book — a table

The old site was a five-year-old template that took nearly six seconds to load on a phone. The reservation link sat three taps deep, the menu was a blurry PDF, and the restaurant didn't appear in local search for "farm-to-table near me." There was no analytics, so no one knew where bookings were leaking.

  • 5.8s mobile load time — visitors bounced before the menu appeared
  • Booking link buried below the fold and three taps deep
  • Invisible in local search; no Google Business integration
  • Menu locked in a downloadable PDF, unreadable on mobile
  • Zero tracking — every marketing dollar was a guess
Our approach

Custom-built around one job: fill more tables

We didn't start a template — we started with the goal. Every design decision pushed toward the reservation. We rebuilt the site mobile-first, made the menu live and crawlable, restructured the pages for local SEO, and added an AI chatbot to answer the questions diners ask before they book.

  • Custom, mobile-first design with a sticky "Reserve a table" button
  • Live, searchable menu replacing the PDF — great for SEO and diners
  • Local SEO + Google Business structure to rank in the neighborhood
  • AI chatbot handling hours, dietary, and party-size questions 24/7
  • Google Analytics with conversion tracking on every booking
