Good to know

Pricing questions, answered

How does pricing work?

The prices shown are transparent starting points. In a free consult we scope your project, then put an exact quote in writing — and that number is the number you pay. If you decide you want more along the way — extra pages, a chatbot, a booking system — we'll quote it as a clear add-on before any work starts. No surprise invoices, ever.

Are the growth tools really included, or are they add-ons?

Analytics and on-page SEO are built into every package, including Starter. The AI chatbot, advanced SEO, and conversion tracking come included from Growth up. We bake the growth tech into the build — it isn't a pricey upsell tacked on at the end.

Do I have to sign up for a monthly Care plan?

No. Care plans are completely optional. Your package includes a support window (1–6 months depending on the tier), and after that you can continue on a Care plan, manage the site yourself, or take it elsewhere. There's no lock-in, and you can cancel anytime.

How long until my site is live?

Most Starter and Growth sites launch in about two weeks from the moment we have your content and a signed-off design. Scale projects with e-commerce or custom features run on a scoped timeline we agree on together up front.

What if I don't love the design?

We revise it until you do. Design revisions are included in every package — you'll never get stuck with a look you don't love. We'd rather take an extra round than ship something you're not proud of.

Can I pay in installments?

Absolutely. Our default is a simple 50% deposit / 50% at launch. You can also split a project into three interest-free monthly payments, or choose a lower-deposit Build + Care bundle that gets you live for less upfront. We'll lay out the options in your quote.
