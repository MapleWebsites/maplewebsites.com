Our story

We're a studio with one big obsession: websites that grow your business.

Maple is a custom web-design and development studio. We don't sell templates or one-size-fits-all packages — we design and build websites tailored to your business, then wire in the growth tools most agencies treat as expensive extras: AI chatbots, Google Analytics, and SEO. Why we started Maple

Great design shouldn't mean a confusing invoice.

Maple started with a frustration we kept hearing from small-business owners: they'd paid for a "professional" website, only to end up with a pretty brochure that never brought in a single lead — and a bill full of mystery line items.

We thought it should work the other way around. A website is your hardest-working employee: it's open 24/7, it greets every visitor, and it should be measurably earning its keep. So we built a studio around that idea — custom design paired with the analytics, SEO, and AI tools that turn visitors into customers.

Today we do one thing, and we do it well: build fast, beautiful, custom websites with growth tech built in — at a transparent, upfront price, usually live in about two weeks.

What makes us different

  • 🎯Custom, never canned. Every site is designed from scratch around your brand and goals.
  • 🧰Growth tools included. Chatbot, analytics, and SEO ship with the site — not as add-ons.
  • 💸Transparent pricing. Clear starting prices, then an exact quote we put in writing — and that's the number you pay. No surprise invoices, ever.
  • ⚡Fast to launch. Most sites go live in roughly two weeks.
